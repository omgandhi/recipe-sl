Hey there!

This is an app where you can save your favourite recipes, and then add the ingredients for these recipes to a shopping list.

To get started, click on the green button to create a new recipe. You can also navigate to the shopping list from clicking the button on the header to add items manually. Clicking "Get Recipes" on the navbar will display a couple recipes that I found online.

Have fun!
